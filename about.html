Our Humble Beginnings

From a Small Kitchen to a Culinary Destination

Founded in 2008 by Chef Antonio Lerro, Delicious Bites began as a small family-owned restaurant with just 10 tables and a dream to share authentic, homemade flavors with our community. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved local institution.

Today, we continue to honor our roots while embracing innovation, creating a dining experience that blends tradition with contemporary culinary artistry. Our commitment to quality, freshness, and exceptional service remains unchanged.

Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape every dish we create and every experience we deliver

Our Mission

To create unforgettable dining experiences by crafting delicious, nutritious meals from the freshest ingredients, served with warmth and hospitality that makes every customer feel like family.

  • Source 90% of ingredients from local farms
  • Reduce food waste by 50% through innovative practices
  • Maintain a 4.8+ customer satisfaction rating

Our Vision

To become the most beloved restaurant brand in the region, recognized for culinary excellence, sustainable practices, and community engagement that enriches lives through food.

  • Expand to 10 locations by 2030
  • Launch a culinary training program for aspiring chefs
  • Achieve carbon neutrality in all operations
